Early recovery is loud. Your thoughts race. Your emotions swing. Your brain won’t shut up now that the chemicals are gone.

After The Noise is a beginner-friendly guide to early recovery written by someone who’s lived it — not a guru, not a clinician, just someone in recovery who’s lost more than he’s gained and had to learn, the hard way, what actually helps and what’s just noise pretending to be wisdom.

Blending straight talk, humor, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), SMART Recovery principles, and grounded philosophy, this book is meant to sit beside you in the early days — when clarity feels distant and everything feels personal.
